Submission Guidelines

We accept work written primarily in English across all genres, including short stories, flash fiction and nonfiction, personal essays, reviews, poetry, scripts, and comics. We also accept art and photography, as well as video and audio submissions.

Contributors may submit once per genre per reading period.

If your work was published in Issue 1, please wait to submit again until Issue 3.

Submissions for Issue 2 will close February 28.

CH will consider one work of prose of up to 4000 words or 2–3 flash prose pieces of up to 1000 words each; up to 5 poems of any length; and up to 5 works of art or photographs, OR an audio or video submission of up to 5 minutes (all multimedia submissions must be accompanied by an artist statement).

All submitted work must be previously unpublished (published works include those posted on personal blogs or social media accounts). CH accepts simultaneous submissions.

Submissions must be made here.
