Submission Guidelines

We accept work written primarily in English across all genres, including short stories, flash fiction and nonfiction, personal essays, reviews, poetry, scripts, and comics. We also accept art and photography, as well as video and audio submissions. We accept submissions on a rolling basis.

The cutoff date for submissions to our Fall 2022 issue is October 31. Submissions received after this date will be considered for our Winter 2023 issue.

  1. We accept electronic submissions only. Please send your submissions to commonhousemag@gmail.com and include your piece’s genre in the subject line.

  2. Please include a cover letter with your name, your piece’s title and word count, and a brief third-person biographical statement.

  3. Please submit your piece(s) as a:
    1. Word document (.doc or .docx) or PDF (.pdf) for written work
    2. PNG file for art and photography
    3. MP3 file for audio
    4. MP4 file for video

  4. The name of your document should be LAST NAME_TITLE, and all pieces should be included in the same document.

  5. Please submit:
    1. One work of prose up to 4000 words or 2–3 flash pieces up to 1000 words each
    2. Up to 5 poems
    3. Up to 5 works of art or photographs
    4. Audio and video submissions of up to 5 minutes

  6. We accept excerpts from longer work, but please indicate that your piece is an excerpt and provide contextual details in your cover letter.

  7. We are interested in original, previously unpublished works. Published works include works posted on personal blogs or social media.

  8. We accept simultaneous submissions, but please let us know as soon as possible if your work has been accepted elsewhere.

  9. You do not need to be a current uOttawa student to submit work. If you are a current student, please indicate so in your email.
